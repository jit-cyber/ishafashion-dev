Our Story

Isha Fashions was established in 2017 by Mr. Sagar Kathuria. Quickly becoming one of the leading manufacturers and exporters of leather apparel, accessories, bags, and belts, we pride ourselves on quality and environmental standards. Our in-house leather tannery allows us to provide complete customized manufacturing and OEM solutions to top brands and fashion houses worldwide. Our commitment to international laws and worker welfare is affirmed through our full SEDEX compliance and HIGG certification for our tannery.

About us

Isha Fashions is a premier manufacturer and exporter of leather apparel and accessories, established in 2017 by Mr. Sagar Kathuria. We specialize in customized manufacturing and OEM solutions for leading global brands. Our own tannery ensures high-quality standards, and our commitment to safety, worker welfare, and environmental compliance is recognized through SEDEX and HIGG certifications.

Our Infrastucture

Our factory meets high customer standards, prioritizing product and worker safety. With state-of-the-art machinery and a skilled workforce of 300, we produce 600 bags daily. The facility includes dedicated production floors, a sampling unit, quality control, and raw material storage, all managed by experienced professionals.
